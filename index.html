About Us

The Project is made using tableau and the data is tored in MYsql Server.The project contains the following:

  • Insights about Sugarcane Production according to the country
  • Insights about Sugar Production according to the continent
  • Insights about Sugarcane Production all over the world

Analyzing sugarcane production data provides valuable insights into the sugar market. This information is used by traders, investors, and policymakers to make predictions about sugar prices and supply-demand dynamics. By staying informed about production trends, market participants can make strategic decisions related to sugar trading, investments, and import/export activities.
